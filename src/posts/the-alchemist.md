## LEGENDARY

What is it that you live for? What is your purpose? Have you ever observed the omens god keeps throwing at you? The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho will certainly point you to where and what you are missing in life. It is not that easy to find your purpose but the more you observe the more you will realise that your Personal Legend has been staring you in the face. The elements in nature, humans, animals, the sun, stars everything speaks to a certain something that is the soul of the world. It is what connects us to everything in life. It is what guides us through and tells us the errors of others as we traverse our own path. We just need to strive to become better than we are, as we evolve the universe evolves too, it gets our teachings and learns from our experience. The treasure in your life doesnt have to be material. But only as you start on your journey to find this treasure you are sure to unlock a whole new world. Some of it may even be better than the one you were living in. You have to set your sights on the big prize because once you do realise your personal legend, life as you know it will be a breeze as you help others realise theirs. Seeing that most humans are blindsided in their journey towards the legend you learn everyones story. There is only one important thing that you should know as you start your journey and that is
**“TO BE A TRUE BELIEVER IN YOUR OWN PERSONAL LEGEND”**
