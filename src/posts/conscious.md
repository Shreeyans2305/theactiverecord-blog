## Conscious
What does it mean to be conscious? Is it the mere ability to think? I dont *think* so. To me, being conscious signifies free thought. Is any AI conscious? Maybe. The dictionary says, consciousness is about being aware of and responding to one’s surroundings. That doesn’t really make sense to me. Any pre-trained robot can be aware of and respond to one’s surrounding, LLM’s can adapt to situations and hold conversations. The definition of being conscious is changing as we speak. What does that mean for us? How about we redefine consciousness to mean, “To be aware of one’s existence and consequences thereof.”  Maybe it’s more fitting to the modern world. 
Are animals conscious? I mean they do know they exist, but how much do they really think? I know that they have emotions, and are smart but does their thinking stretch beyond the concept of survival? Can they be creative, can they think about science, hell can they think about consciousness itself?
Say we simulate a world entirely mimicking our own, with it’s own environment, space, animals, humans, relationships, stimuli, etc. Would the people in that world be conscious? Or would they simply be a string of 1’s and 0’s acting out consciousness. To them, they would surely be, but to us? They’re just electricity. **Electricity can think it’s conscious!**
_So I ask you this dear reader, are you conscious?_
